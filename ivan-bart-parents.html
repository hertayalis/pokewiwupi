Who Are Ivan Bart Parents?: Ivan Bart, a beloved figure in fashion and a modeling pioneer has reportedly passed away

The news of his death was announced in an Instagram post to his account on Saturday, 28 October 2023. It featured a portrait of Bart with the caption: “Our world has lost one of the greats”

The former president of IMG Models, Ivan Bart died at 60 on Friday, 27 October 2023 after a short illness. His death was confirmed by Mark Shapiro, president and chief operating officer of Endeavor, which owns IMG Models.

Who Are Ivan Bart Parents?

Ivan Bart hardly opened up about his family hence we do not have any information regarding his parents. His parents’ name, date of birth, age, and occupation are currently unknown.

Bart first joined IMG Models in 1994 and served as president from 2014. He later stepped down but remained a senior advisor.

Since joining the IMG as president, Bart played a powerful role in pushing for size diversity in the industry.

During three decades at IMG Models, he guided the careers of many of the most successful models in the industry and also changed the shape and face of the modeling industry

Bart was behind some of the biggest faces of the industry. Throughout his career, he worked with models such as Carolyn Murphy, Paloma Elsesser, Precious Lee, Stephanie Seymour, Ashley Graham, Halima Aden, and Hari Nef.
