Katie Holmes Proves You Can Pair Your Button-Down With Sweatpants

 Posted on June 2, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  305 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Katie Holmes is perfecting lazy-girl dressing one outfit at a time.

This week, the Rare Objects director stepped out in New York City in a loose light-blue button-down, which she wore over a gray tank and unexpectedly paired with cozy white sweatpants from Alo. To finish the look, she opted for black Adidas Samba sneakers, a black leather tote bag, large round sunglasses in a dark brown, and dainty gold jewelry. She seemed to wear no makeup and had her freshly washed hair down.
