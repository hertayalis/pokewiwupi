Lana Del Rey Reveals Why She Doesn't Have Kids &amp; Her Future Plans

 Posted on September 8, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  313 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Lana Del Rey recently got candid about her family planning journey in an unexpectedly intimate interview. The 38-year-old singer and famed Waffle House “employee” told The Sunday Times she doesn’t have kids because there is “more to explore” in her life.

“That’s why God didn’t give me children yet,” she said. “…I know people who’ve tested every water. It’s burnt them, like Icarus. But I’m willing to go there.”
