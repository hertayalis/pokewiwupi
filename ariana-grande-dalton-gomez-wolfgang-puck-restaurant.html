Ariana Grande and Dalton Gomez Out at Wolfgang Puck's Restaurant

 Posted on June 22, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  387 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Ariana Grande and Dalton Gomez made their first post-wedding, public appearance together on Saturday night.

The couple were seen with four friends at Wolfgang Puck's restaurant at Hotel Bel-Air in Los Angeles. Puck posed with the group for a photo, which he posted to his Instagram yesterday evening, writing in the caption, "Always a pleasuring having good company at @hotelbelair! Thank you for coming by @arianagrande!"

Grande and Gomez, a real estate agent, are reportedly doing extremely well following their nuptials last month. "Ari and Dalton were very giddy throughout the dinner and were cuddling close the entire time," an insider told E! News of the couple's group date. "You could tell they were in love and she was very happy. Ari mentioned that she was excited to be celebrating finally being married."

The source further shared that "Dalton had a huge smile on his face throughout the night and they definitely looked to be in the honeymoon phase and so in love," adding that the couple and their friends spent the entire evening at the hotel and were "super excited to meet Wolfgang Puck and were all talking about the food experience."

Following their five-month engagement, which the singer officially confirmed via an Instagram post last December, Grande and Gomez tied the knot on May 15 with an intimate wedding of less than 20 attendees.

The couple began dating last January, confirming their relationship with the public via a joint appearance in Justin Bieber's "Stuck with U" quarantine music video. Since then, the pair have reportedly enjoyed growing more intimate with one another "in peace" throughout quarantine, away from the external pressures of the spotlight. "This phase of her life has been very quiet and uneventful in a good way," a source told People.

"Dalton is a great guy. He is very focused on work and low-key," a source previously told the outlet. "He likes keeping his relationship with Ari private. They have been able to get to know each other in peace."

The source also shared, "Ari's family is very happy. Everyone loves Dalton. He is great for Ari."
