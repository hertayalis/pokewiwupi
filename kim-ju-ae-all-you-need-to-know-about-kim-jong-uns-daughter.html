Kim Ju-ae, All You Need To Know About Kim Jong-un's daughter

 Posted on June 3, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  507 words  |  Martina Birk

Kim Ju-ae is the daughter of North Korean Supreme Leader Kim Jong-un and First Lady of North Korea Ri Sol-Ju. Kim Jue-ae’s father, Kim Jong-un and his family have been characterized as “secretive”.  Very little information about their daughter has appeared in official North Korean sources, but more speculative information has been reported in foreign media.

Kim Ju-ae is the daughter of the supreme Leader of North Korea. She was born in 2013 making her 8 years old as of 2021. The exact day of birth have been difficult to pin down, but can be roughly inferred from the lengthy periods in which Ri, Ju-ae’s mother mysteriously vanished from public life. Little is known about the girl and her family as they are very secretive about family matters. And even less is known about the three young children the couple is believed to have.

Most of the currently available information about Kim Ju-ae and the family comes from South Korea’s National Intelligence Service. But former US basketball star Dennis Rodman has also spoken to media about holding one of Kim’s children during one of his controversial visits to the Hermit Kingdom, though the details he provided have not been independently corroborated.

The leader of the North Korean country’s first child is believed to be a boy, born in 2010, and his second child a daughter- Kim Ju-ae born in 2013. The fact that Kim has at least one male heir likely means that he could eventually succeed Kim to continue the family’s dynasty.

The gender of Kim Ju-ae’s other sibling remains unknown. The child is believed to have been born in February 2017. There are no public photos of Kim and Ri’s children, but the couple do occasionally pose for propaganda photos with other people’s kids, like in this photo at a children’s hospital in 2014.
