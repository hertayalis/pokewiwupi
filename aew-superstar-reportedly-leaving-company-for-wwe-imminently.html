AEW Superstar Reportedly Leaving Company for WWE Imminently

 Posted on June 24, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  408 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

WWE and AEW are always looking for a star that can really add dept to their roster. Since Triple H took over WWE’s creative direction, he has brought back several former Superstars, and it appears that one more is coming back.

Andrade decided to leave WWE, but he was able to make his way to AEW in short time. In the years since Andrade joined Tony Khan’s company, things didn’t seem to pick up for him. Now a new report says that he could be on his way out.

WWE has a lot of plans for 2024, and a returning champion was teased for their Day 1 RAW. Many fans have speculated who might fill that spot. Andrade is now a viable option.

WrestlePurists reported that they have heard from AEW sources that Andrade is leaving AEW. It was also noted that some believe that Andrade is the returning Superstar for the Day 1 WWE RAW on January 1st.

According to WrestlePurists sources, Andrade El Idolo is expected by many in AEW to finish up with the company tonight at World’s End.

He is also believed by many within AEW to be heading back to WWE. Some think it could happen as soon as the Day 1 RAW.

A WWE source we spoken to is aware of the talk surrounding Andrade and is expecting him to join the company imminently.

It was previously noted that Anrade might have more time tacked onto the end of his AEW contract due to injury. It appears that might not be the case after all.
