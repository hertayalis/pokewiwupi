It’s Christmastime, and many people all over are getting into the Holiday spirit. Michelle McCool is doing what she can to stay in the Holiday spirit. That being said, it can still be a bit difficult to accomplish that.

We will have to see what happens next for Michelle McCool. She seems to be having a good time for the holidays, even if there were some bumps in the road.
